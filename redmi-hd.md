# Redmi HD – Stock Market Analysis (Simple Version)

## Buy / Sell Signals

| Indicator | Buy Signal | Sell Signal |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **RSI** | Below 30 (oversold) | Above 70 (overbought) |
| **Moving Average** | Price above 50 MA | Price below 50 MA |
| **MACD** | Line crosses above signal line | Line crosses below signal line |
| **Volume** | High volume with price up | High volume with price down |

## Simple Rules

1. **Buy** when 2 or more indicators show Buy
2. **Sell** when 2 or more indicators show Sell
3. **Hold** when mixed signals

## Example

| Stock | RSI | MA | MACD | Volume | Decision |
|-------|-----|----|------|--------|----------|
| RELIANCE | 32 | Above | Buy signal | High | **BUY** |
| TATA | 75 | Below | Sell signal | High | **SELL** |
| HDFC | 55 | Above | No signal | Normal | **HOLD** |

## Risk Tip

Always use **Stop Loss** (3-5% below buy price).
